About Us

Created in Poland in 1926 by four Grzegorczyk sisters: Wanda, Iga, Lucja and Dominika, called the WILD sisters. Our circus has always been growing over the generations, giving more and more of their passion and love for circus. Each has her speciality that they transmitted to their children and grand-children. The family knows how to mix tradition and innovation to pleased the most of people and so has acquired an international renown !
